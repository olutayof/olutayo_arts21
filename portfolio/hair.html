Navigating the Workplace with Black Hair


The project was a mobile app that would sevre as an online community for Black women to share their lived experiences regarding their hair at different parts of their professional journey.
The project incorporated user interviews and original illustrations to create an authentic narrative.
The app was an exercise in developing unique mixed media content and following the complete design process including ideation, content planning, research and development.
